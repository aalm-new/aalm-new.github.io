Effective date: September 22, 2025

We respect your privacy. Our apps are built to work with minimal data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • On‑device settings. Things like duration, sounds, colors, and background selections are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.
  • Media you pick. If you choose a custom audio track or background from Files or your photo library, the app reads that file on your device. We do not upload your media.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics. Apple may provide aggregated, anonymized crash reports and performance data if you opted in to share analytics with developers. We use these only to improve stability.
  • Support emails. If you email us, we receive your email address and any details you include. We use this only to help you and to improve the app.

What we do not collect

  • No account is required.
  • No location tracking.
  • No third‑party advertising SDKs.
  • No sale of personal data.

Payments

Payments and trials are handled by Apple through in‑app purchases. We do not receive your full payment details.

How we use data

  • Provide core features like timers, sounds, and backgrounds.
  • Fix bugs and improve performance using anonymized diagnostics.
  • Respond to your messages and support requests.

Data retention

Settings stay on your device until you delete the app. Support emails are kept as long as needed to assist you and meet legal or accounting needs, then deleted.

Your choices

  • Remove local data by deleting the app.
  • Opt out of sharing diagnostics in iOS Settings.
  • Email us to request deletion of support emails related to you.

Children

Our apps are for general audiences and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will change the effective date above and, if changes are significant, we will note them in the app or on this page.
