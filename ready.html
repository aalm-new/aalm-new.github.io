How it works

Ready reads your Apple Health metrics each morning and gives you a clear readiness signal:

  • Green — Time to push harder. Your body is primed for peak performance.
  • Yellow — Maintain and listen. Focus on technique, not intensity.
  • Red — Active recovery day. Rest, mobility, or light walking.

Your readiness score (0–100) is calculated from HRV, resting heart rate, sleep quality, and respiratory rate using personalised z-score thresholds — no generic benchmarks.
